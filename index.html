HOW IT WORKS

simple
1

Make

Open Geedo Studio. Draw frame-by-frame on a 128×64 canvas. Onion-skin, fonts, image import, the works.

2

Publish

Drop the .geedo.json into the hub. It's packed into a tiny binary and shipped to GitHub Pages.

3

Sync

Every Geedo on Wi-Fi polls the hub once a minute. New animations appear automatically. No reflashing.
