What is Geedo?

A handheld ESP32 pet you can program, animate, and customize

01

Make Animations

Use Geedo Studio — a pixel-perfect 128×64 animator with onion skinning, image import, and Floyd-Steinberg dithering.

02

Share with Friends

Publish public, send Private Codes only your friends can unlock, or schedule Time Capsules that pop on a future date.

03

Discover Rare Drops

Some animations are Random Discoveries — they unlock on your Geedo at random. Trade codes with friends to collect them all.

Unlock Systems

Three ways to get new stuff onto your Geedo

⏳

Time Capsules

Drops that unlock on a specific date. Birthday surprises, holiday specials, countdown drops.

★

Random Discoveries

Rare animations that pop into your Geedo at random times. Some are super rare — keep checking!

⚿

Private Codes

Make an animation just for your friends. They enter a 6-letter code on the Hub and it unlocks on their Geedo.
